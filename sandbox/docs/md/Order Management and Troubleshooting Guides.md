# Order Management and Troubleshooting Guides

These guides are designed to help users of the QuickBite food delivery app manage their orders effectively and troubleshoot common issues. They are written in clear, step-by-step language to empower users to resolve problems independently while providing escalation paths to customer support. QuickBite connects users with local restaurants and independent delivery partners for seamless food ordering and delivery.

All guides assume you're using the latest version of the QuickBite app (available on iOS and Android). If you're experiencing app crashes or technical glitches, update the app or restart your device first.

## 1. Order Placement Guide

This guide covers how to place, edit, or cancel an order. Orders are processed in real-time, so act quickly for changes.

**Step-by-Step: Placing an Order**

1. **Open the App and Browse**: Launch QuickBite, allow location access for nearby restaurants, or search by cuisine, restaurant name, or dish (e.g., "pizza in Delhi").
2. **Select Items**: Tap on a restaurant, browse the menu, and add items to your cart. Customize options (e.g., spice level, extras) as available.
3. **Review Cart**: Check your cart for accuracy, including quantities, customizations, and any applied promotions (e.g., "Buy 1 Get 1" or coupon codes).
4. **Enter Delivery Details**: Confirm or update your delivery address, add instructions (e.g., "Leave at gate"), and select a delivery time if scheduled orders are available.
5. **Apply Payments and Fees**: Choose a payment method (card, UPI, wallet), review fees (delivery, service, taxes), and add a tip for the delivery partner.
6. **Place Order**: Tap "Place Order." You'll receive a confirmation with an estimated preparation and delivery time (typically 30-45 minutes).

**Editing an Order**

- Edits are possible only before the restaurant accepts (usually within 1-2 minutes).
- From the order status screen: Tap "Edit Order" > Make changes (add/remove items) > Confirm. Additional charges may apply.
- If the restaurant has accepted, edits are not possible—contact support via in-app chat for assistance.

**Canceling an Order** (Refer to Cancellation Policy for refund details)

- Within 2 minutes of placement: Go to order status > Tap "Cancel" > Select reason > Confirm. Full refund processed automatically.
- After acceptance: Cancellations are rare but possible if preparation hasn't started. Contact support immediately; fees may apply if food is wasted.

**Tips for Smooth Placement**:

- Use saved addresses and payment methods for faster checkout.
- During peak hours (e.g., evenings), expect surge pricing—check alternatives if needed.
- For group orders, use the "Share Cart" feature to collaborate with friends.

## 2. Tracking and Status Explanations Guide

Track your order in real-time via the app's "Orders" tab. Push notifications keep you updated.

**Order Status Definitions and Actions**
Use this table for quick reference:

| Status               | Description                                                   | Estimated Time               | What to Do If Stuck                                                                    |
| -------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Order Placed**     | Your order is submitted and awaiting restaurant confirmation. | Immediate                    | Wait 2-5 minutes. If no update, refresh app or check internet.                         |
| **Accepted**         | Restaurant has confirmed and started preparing.               | 5-10 minutes after placement | Monitor progress. No edits/cancellations possible now.                                 |
| **Preparing**        | Food is being cooked/packaged.                                | 10-30 minutes                | Relax—updates are automatic. If delayed >15 minutes beyond estimate, contact support.  |
| **Ready for Pickup** | Order is prepared; waiting for delivery partner assignment.   | 2-5 minutes                  | If no partner assigned in 10 minutes, app will notify; support can reassign.           |
| **Out for Delivery** | Delivery partner has picked up and is en route.               | 10-20 minutes to arrival     | Track live on map. Contact partner via masked call/chat if needed (e.g., gate access). |
| **Delivered**        | Order handed over; delivery complete.                         | N/A                          | Rate the experience! Report issues within 60 minutes for refunds.                      |
| **Canceled**         | Order stopped (by you, restaurant, or QuickBite).             | N/A                          | Check email/app for refund status. Reorder if needed.                                  |

**Troubleshooting Tracking Issues**

- **No Updates**: Ensure notifications are enabled (Settings > Notifications). Refresh the app or log out/in.
- **Map Not Loading**: Check GPS permissions and internet. If persistent, switch to "List View" for text updates.
- **Delayed Beyond Estimate**: Factors like traffic or high demand can cause this. If >30 minutes late, tap "Help" on the order screen for automated credit or support chat.
- **Wrong Location Shown**: Verify your address in profile. If partner's location is off, contact support—they can reroute.

## 3. Missing/Wrong Items Protocol

If your order arrives incomplete or incorrect, report it promptly for quick resolution.

**Step-by-Step Reporting and Resolution**

1. **Inspect Immediately**: Upon delivery, check all items against your order receipt (accessible in-app).
2. **Document the Issue**: Take clear photos of the packaging, missing/wrong items, and receipt.
3. **Report in App**: Go to "Orders" > Select the order > Tap "Report Issue" > Choose "Missing/Wrong Items" > Upload photos and describe (e.g., "Burger missing fries").
4. **Submit and Wait**: Our AI support reviews in 1-5 minutes. You'll get a response via notification.
5. **Resolution Options**:
   - **Partial Refund**: Credited to your payment method or QuickBite wallet (e.g., value of missing item + 10% goodwill).
   - **Replacement**: If feasible, a new delivery (free) within 30-60 minutes.
   - **Full Refund**: For major errors (e.g., entirely wrong order).
6. **Follow-Up**: If unsatisfied, escalate to live chat or email support@quickbite.com.

**Prevention Tips**:

- Double-check customizations during placement.
- For frequent issues with a restaurant, leave a review to help others.
- Note: Taste preferences (e.g., "too spicy") are not eligible for refunds unless specified as an allergen.

## 4. Allergen and Dietary Info Handling Guide

QuickBite prioritizes food safety. Restaurants provide info, but always verify if you have allergies.

**Managing Allergens and Dietary Preferences**

- **During Order Placement**:
  1. Search for "allergen-free" or dietary tags (e.g., vegan, gluten-free) in menus.
  2. Add notes in "Special Instructions" (e.g., "Severe nut allergy—ensure no cross-contamination").
  3. Restaurants may reject orders if they can't accommodate.
- **Accessing Info**: Tap "Restaurant Info" > "Menu Details" for allergen disclaimers (e.g., "May contain traces of dairy"). If unclear, contact the restaurant via app chat before ordering.
- **Troubleshooting Issues**:
  - **Suspected Allergen Exposure**: Stop consuming immediately. Report via "Report Issue" > "Food Safety/Allergen" with symptoms and photos. We'll prioritize and may offer medical reimbursement up to ₹5,000 (subject to verification).
  - **Dietary Mismatch**: If food doesn't match tags (e.g., non-veg in veg order), follow Missing/Wrong Items Protocol for refund/replacement.
  - **Post-Delivery Concerns**: For non-urgent queries, use in-app FAQ or support.

**Important Disclaimer**: QuickBite relies on restaurant-provided info; we cannot guarantee 100% accuracy. Users with severe allergies should consult restaurants directly or avoid ordering.
