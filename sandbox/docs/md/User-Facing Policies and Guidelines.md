# User-Facing Policies and Guidelines

Here is a detailed set of **User-Facing Policies and Guidelines** for a fictitious food delivery app called **"QuickBite"**. These are written in clear, user-friendly language while incorporating standard legal protections and common practices from the food delivery industry (inspired by platforms like Zomato, Swiggy, Uber Eats, DoorDash, and others).

QuickBite is a platform that connects users with restaurants and independent delivery partners to facilitate ordering and delivery of food and other items.

### 2. Privacy Policy

### 3. Cancellation and Refund Policy

**3.1 Order Cancellation by User**

- You may cancel an order **within 2 minutes** of placement (before the restaurant accepts it) — full refund (minus any payment gateway fees).
- After restaurant acceptance or preparation starts, cancellations are generally not possible.
- In exceptional cases (e.g., long delays), contact support for possible cancellation/refund.

**3.2 Cancellation by Restaurant or QuickBite**

- If a restaurant cannot fulfill an order, we cancel and issue a **full refund** (including delivery fees) within 3–7 business days.
- If delivery fails due to our or the delivery partner's fault, full refund.

**3.3 Refund Eligibility**

- **Full refund**: Order not prepared/accepted, wrong item delivered, order significantly late (>60 minutes beyond estimated time due to our fault), spoiled/unfit food.
- **Partial refund**: Missing items, quality issues (at our discretion, often 20–100% of affected items).
- No refunds for change of mind after preparation, taste preferences, or minor issues.
- Refunds issued to original payment method (or platform credit in some cases). Processing time: 5–10 business days.

**3.4 Delivery and Service Issues**

- If food arrives cold/spoiled due to delivery delay beyond our control (e.g., traffic/weather), we may offer partial credit.
- Report issues within 60 minutes of delivery with photos if possible.

**3.5 Promotions and Credits**  
Refunds on promotional/discount orders may return as platform credits.

### 4. Delivery and Service Fees Policy

**4.1 Fees Explained**

- **Delivery Fee**: Based on distance, time, demand (dynamic). Shown at checkout.
- **Service Fee**: Platform charge for technology, support, etc. (typically 10–20%).
- **Small Order Fee**: Applied for orders below a threshold (e.g., ₹150).
- **Surge/Peak Pricing**: Higher fees during high demand.
- **Taxes & Packaging**: As charged by restaurant/government.
- **Tips**: Optional; 100% goes to delivery partner.

**4.2 No Hidden Fees**  
All charges are displayed before payment. We do not add undisclosed fees post-checkout.

These policies form the core user-facing legal framework for QuickBite. They should be linked prominently in the app (e.g., footer, checkout screen, account settings) and require explicit acceptance during signup or first order.
